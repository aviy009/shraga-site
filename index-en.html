The Problem

Israeli insurance agencies run on spreadsheets and phone calls

The market is fragmented across dozens of disconnected insurer portals, each with its own login, its own format, its own quirks. No unified workflow. No automation. Just people.

  • Renewals tracked manually — clients lapse before anyone notices
  • No bandwidth for proactive outreach — agents are always reactive
  • The only way to grow is to hire more people — margins never improve
The Solution

An AI agent that actually understands insurance — in Hebrew

Shraga isn't a generic AI wrapper. It's trained on insurance domain knowledge, connected to the real systems agents use every day, and capable of completing tasks end-to-end without human intervention.

  • Monitors every policy and acts before renewals are missed
  • Runs marketing campaigns autonomously — right client, right time
  • One agent handles 3× more clients — no new hires needed
